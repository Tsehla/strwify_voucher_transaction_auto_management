Uwireless is a connectivity solution company, developed with a sole purpose of creating unique high quality low cost products and services to meets the needs of todays businesses and general public.

The Intention still holds true even now, except now we have enlarged our vission to include other wireless connectivity solutions personalised to meet varius unique businesses wants and needs.
